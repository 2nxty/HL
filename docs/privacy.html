Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Privacy First: HyLauncher is designed as an offline launcher. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Short answer: None.

HyLauncher operates entirely on your local machine in offline mode. We do not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics from the launcher application itself.

2. Website Analytics

Our website (hylauncher.net) may use basic analytics to understand visitor traffic. This may include:

  • Anonymous page views and visit duration
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region level only)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Referral sources

This data is aggregated and anonymous. We cannot identify individual users from this information.

3. Downloads

When you download HyLauncher, the hosting provider may log:

  • Download timestamp
  • IP address (for security purposes)
  • File accessed

This information is used solely to prevent abuse and ensure download availability. We do not use it for tracking or marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

HyLauncher may interact with the following external services:

  • Discord: If you join our community Discord server, Discord's privacy policy applies
  • GitHub: If we host on GitHub, their privacy policy applies to repository interactions
  • CDN Providers: For faster downloads, subject to their privacy policies

5. Cookies

Our website may use minimal cookies for:

  • Remembering your preferences (theme, language)
  • Basic analytics (if enabled)

No cookies are used for advertising or cross-site tracking.

6. Data Storage and Security

HyLauncher stores game data and configuration files locally on your computer. This includes:

  • Game installation files
  • Launcher settings and preferences
  • Offline player profiles (if created)

All data remains on your device. We have no access to it.

7. Children's Privacy

HyLauncher does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information at all, this is inherently protected. Parents should supervise their children's internet usage.

8. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to request, delete, or export. You maintain full control over all local data stored by the launcher.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Open Source Transparency

HyLauncher is community-developed. Users are encouraged to review the source code to verify our privacy claims and ensure no unauthorized data collection occurs.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or data practices, please reach out through our Discord community.

Final note: HyLauncher respects your privacy. We don't collect your data because we don't need it. Your gaming experience is yours alone.
